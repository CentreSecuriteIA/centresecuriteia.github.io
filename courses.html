Discover academic courses, reading groups, and organized programs using the AI Safety Atlas materials. Join an existing course or start your own with our resources.

Official Certificates

Official LinkedIn certificates available for participants who complete course requirements. The specific requirements vary by the course provider.

Common Certificate Requirements:

  • Attend at least 80% of the sessions
  • Read the textbook, and participate actively in group discussions
  • Submit final projects or equivalent contribution
